Davis Centennial Seal Proposal

For this project I want to explore an interactive design that allows users to explore the city of Davis through some of the elements found on the centennial seal. The idea is to create a map of the city and design a treasure hunt in which users locate different areas within the map and learn about the different elements of the seal as they explore Davis. Parts of the seal would be revealed as the user explores key locations and the completion of the map results in the completion of the seal.
